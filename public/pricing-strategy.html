Section 5 — The 6 Pricing Plays
How to apply each one to your 4 pubs
⚓
1. Anchoring
Show a bigger number first so yours feels small. Lead with what an agency charges. Then reveal yours.
"Most reputation management agencies charge £400–£800/month. We do it for £55 because we built our own AI — not a reseller."
🔒
2. Founder Rate Lock-in
First 10 clients get a special rate — £45/month (locked forever, never increases). Creates urgency. Creates loyalty. Creates referrals.
"You'd be one of our first 10 clients — so you get our Founder Rate: £45/month instead of £55, locked in for life even as we grow."
📅
3. Annual Commitment Discount
Offer a 2-month discount for paying 12 months upfront. You get cash. They get saving. You reduce churn to near zero.
"Pay for 10 months, get 12 — that's £550/year instead of £660. Save £110 and lock in today's rate."
🎯
4. Pain Pricing
Calculate what it costs them NOT to have you. Show that number first. Then your price is tiny by comparison.
"2 lost tables a week × £45 spend = £90/week gone. That's £4,680 a year. We fix it for £55 a month."
⏰
5. Scarcity + Urgency
5 onboarding spots per month. Real (you can't set up 20 clients well in a month). Creates decision urgency without being fake.
"We only onboard 5 pubs a month so setup quality stays high. I've got 2 spots left in April — after that it's May."
🔁
6. Risk Reversal
30-day money-back on the monthly fee. Setup fee is the work already done — non-refundable. Removes objection risk while protecting your income.
"If you're not happy after 30 days I'll refund the £55 — no questions. The setup fee covers the 3 hours I've already spent building it for you."
