Section 4 — Where You Win & Lose
Every case, brutally honest
✅ Win
Client 1 pays £350 + stays 3 months
Total in: £515. Total out: £98 (setup) + £38 (2mo ongoing) = £136. Profit: £379. Win even on early churn.
✅ Win
5 clients steady — no new ones
£189/month pure recurring. £2,268/year doing nothing except support. Fixed costs fully absorbed from client 3.
✅ Win
Client invokes 30-day guarantee
You refund £55 MRR. You keep £350 setup. Net day-1 profit: £290. The setup fee is your insurance policy.
✅ Win
2 clients, 1 churns month 2
Month 1: +£307. Month 2: +£350 (C2 setup covers it). Month 3 onwards: £103/mo on just 1 steady client.
❌ Lose
Client demands full refund incl. setup
You refund £405. You spent £60 time + £26 fixed + £12 variable = £98. Net loss: £98 − £0 = you lose £98. One bad client max. Non-refundable T&Cs = this never happens.
❌ Lose
0 clients — 3 months in
You pay £26/mo fixed for 3 months = £78 total loss. That's it. Your downside is capped at £78 if you get zero traction in 3 months. Not a money risk. A motivation risk.
⚠️ Watch Out
1–2 clients only — low margin
With just 1 client ongoing you keep £17/month. Fixed costs dominate. At 1–2 clients you're covering costs not building income. Push through to 3+ clients as fast as possible.
⚠️ Watch Out
Support time creep past 30 min
If a difficult client needs 2hrs/month = £40 cost not £10. Margin on that client drops from £43 to £13. Set boundaries: Telegram only, response within 24hrs, no WhatsApp DMs.
