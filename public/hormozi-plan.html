Cold email formula
›
4-line formula that works:

Line 1: Name the specific pain (the actual review)
Line 2: Cost of doing nothing
Line 3: What we do in one sentence
Line 4: One low-friction CTA

Example:
"Saw a 1-star review on The Crown and Shuttle sitting unanswered for 3 days.
Every day it sits there, someone reads it and picks a different pub.
We reply in your voice automatically — you just tap approve.
Worth 15 minutes? 0-clicks.uk/call"
→ Every email follows this structure. No exceptions.

🎯
The 15-min demo call script
›
Don't pitch. Show.

Min 0–2: "Tell me about your busiest review period — when do most come in?"

Min 2–5: Pull up their actual Google reviews live on screen. Find a recent one. Say: "Watch this."

Min 5–10: Run it through Zero Clicks live. Show the AI reply in their voice. Ask: "Is that how you'd reply?"

Min 10–13: If yes → "That happens automatically, the second every review lands. You just tap approve on Telegram."

Min 13–15: "Want me to set it up today? Free for 14 days."

Close rate: 50%+. The product sells itself when they see it work on their own pub.
→ Never pitch features. Always demo on their actual reviews.
